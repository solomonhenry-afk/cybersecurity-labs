# Firewall Rule Governance Policy

## Purpose
Establish mandatory requirements for firewall rule creation, review, and retirement.

## Policy Requirements
- Every rule must have a documented business justification
- An accountable owner must be assigned
- Rules must follow least privilege principles
- Expiration dates are mandatory unless explicitly exempted

## Review Cadence
- High-risk rules: Quarterly
- Standard rules: Annually
- Temporary rules: Time-bound with automatic expiration

## Approval Authority
- Security Engineering approves high-risk rules
- Business owners approve functional necessity

## Enforcement
Rules that do not meet policy requirements are subject to removal.
