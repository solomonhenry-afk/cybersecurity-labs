# NAC Access Policy

## Authentication Requirements
- Corporate identity provider
- Multi-factor authentication

## Device Compliance Requirements
- Managed device enrollment
- Approved OS and patch level
- Active endpoint protection

## Enforcement Actions
- Allow: compliant and trusted
- Restrict: limited access for partial compliance
- Quarantine: remediation required
- Deny: failed authentication or high risk

## Exception Handling
Exceptions require documented approval and expiration.
