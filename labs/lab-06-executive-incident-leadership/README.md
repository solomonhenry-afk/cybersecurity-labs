##  Lab Objective

Demonstrate the ability to:

* Lead an incident response at the **executive and board level**
* Balance **technical risk, business impact, legal exposure, and reputation**
* Communicate clearly with **non-technical stakeholders**
* Make time-sensitive decisions with incomplete information

##  Scenario Overview

A confirmed intrusion escalates from:

* Initial access
* Lateral movement
* Potential data exposure

Security leadership must:

* Coordinate SOC, IR, Legal, and Executive teams
* Decide on containment vs. business continuity
* Prepare for regulatory and board scrutiny
