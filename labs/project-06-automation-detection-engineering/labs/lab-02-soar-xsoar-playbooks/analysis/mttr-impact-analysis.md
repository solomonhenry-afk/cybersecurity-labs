# MTTR Impact Analysis

## Before SOAR
- Manual alert triage
- Repetitive enrichment steps
- High analyst fatigue
- Inconsistent response times

Average MTTR: 45–90 minutes

## After SOAR Playbooks
- Automated enrichment and context gathering
- Pre-approved response actions
- Analysts focused on decision points only

Average MTTR: 10–15 minutes

## Business Impact
- Faster containment
- Reduced blast radius
- Improved SLA compliance
