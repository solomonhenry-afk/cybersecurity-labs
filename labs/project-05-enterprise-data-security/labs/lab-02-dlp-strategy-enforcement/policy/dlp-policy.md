# Data Loss Prevention Policy

## Purpose
To prevent unauthorized disclosure of sensitive data while enabling business operations.

## Policy Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and systems handling sensitive data.

## Enforcement Principles
- Risk-based controls
- Least restrictive enforcement required to reduce risk
- Full audit logging and incident response integration

## Compliance Alignment
- GDPR Articles 5, 32
- HIPAA Security Rule
- PCI-DSS data protection requirements
