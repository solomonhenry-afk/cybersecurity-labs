# Encryption & Key Management Policy

## Purpose
Establish mandatory encryption and key management requirements to protect sensitive data.

## Policy Requirements
- All sensitive data must be encrypted at rest and in transit
- Keys must be managed centrally using approved KMS/HSM solutions
- Manual key handling is prohibited

## Key Management Controls
- Key rotation at defined intervals
- Access restricted by role and business need
- All key access logged and monitored

## Compliance Alignment
- GDPR Article 32 (Security of Processing)
- HIPAA Security Rule
- NIST SP 800-53 (SC-12, SC-13, KM)
- CIS Critical Security Controls

## Exceptions
- Exceptions require documented risk acceptance
- Time-bound approvals with executive sign-off
