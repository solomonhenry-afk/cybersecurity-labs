# Breach Notification Policy

This policy defines organizational requirements for breach notification.

Authority:
- CISO confirms technical breach status
- Legal determines notification obligation
- Executive leadership authorizes disclosures

Requirements:
- Accurate scope validation
- Evidence preservation
- Controlled communication channels

Notifications must:
- Be factual and timely
- Avoid speculation
- Align with regulatory guidance

Failure to follow this policy may result in
regulatory penalties and legal exposure.
