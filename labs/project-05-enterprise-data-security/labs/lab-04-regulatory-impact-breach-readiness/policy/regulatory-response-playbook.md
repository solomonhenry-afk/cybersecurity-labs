# Regulatory Response Playbook

Day 0–1:
- Contain incident
- Preserve evidence
- Notify legal and executive leadership

Day 2–3:
- Complete impact assessment
- Determine notification obligations
- Prepare regulator communications

Day 4+:
- Submit notifications if required
- Engage regulators proactively
- Begin remediation and monitoring

Communication Principles:
- Single authoritative voice
- Legal-reviewed messaging
- Transparent and factual updates

This playbook ensures calm, defensible response under pressure.
